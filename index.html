Edison Wu is a Graphics Intensive journalist at The New York Times, where he uses data, graphics, and code to tell stories.

Previously, he worked at Bloomberg, The Texas Tribune, the Investigative Reporting Project, Richmond Confidential and The Beijing News.

He earned his master's degree from the University of California, Berkeley, Graduate School of Journalism.

He speaks English and Chinese. In his spare time, he produces podcasts for the award-winning outlet Initium Media. His team was a finalist for the 2026 SOPA Awards.
